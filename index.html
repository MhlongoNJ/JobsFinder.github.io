Overview: Jobs Finder is a free South African job-browsing app with a built-in CV builder. All personal data you enter is stored locally on your device only and is never uploaded to our servers. We use Google AdMob to serve ads that keep the app free.
📥
Section 1
Information We Collect

Information You Provide

The App allows you to create a personal CV profile. This may include your full name, email address, phone number, location, work experience, education history, skills, languages, references, and a profile photo selected from your device gallery.

Important: This information is stored locally on your device only. It is never transmitted to our servers or any third party by the App itself.

Information Collected Automatically

Jobs Finder does not independently collect usage analytics, crash reports, or behavioural data. The App does not track how you use it.

Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party services that may collect data according to their own policies:

📢
Google AdMob

Serves in-app advertisements. May collect device identifiers, IP address and ad interaction data to serve relevant ads.

View Google Privacy Policy →
🔎
JobMail

Job listings are fetched from jobmail.co.za. A network request is made to their servers when loading jobs. No personal data is shared with JobMail.

Visit JobMail →

Section 2
How We Use Your Information

Information you enter into the CV Builder is used solely to display your CV within the App and to generate a PDF export on your device.

Your job alert keywords and location preferences are stored locally and used only to match and highlight relevant jobs within the App and to trigger local notifications on your device.

Bookmarks, application tracking entries, and browsing history (recently viewed jobs) are stored locally to personalise your in-app experience.

🗄️
Section 3
Data Storage

All personal data you enter — including CV details, bookmarks, application tracking records and preferences — is stored locally on your device using a private encrypted SQLite database (Android Room).

✅  Not uploaded to any cloud server
✅  Not accessible to other apps on your device
✅  Automatically deleted if you uninstall the App

Job listings cached for offline viewing are fetched from JobMail's public website and are also stored locally. They do not contain any of your personal information.

🔔
Section 4
Notifications

The App may send you local push notifications to alert you about new job listings that match your saved keywords and location preferences. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not involve transmitting your personal data to any external server.

You can disable notifications at any time through your device's system settings under Apps → Jobs Finder → Notifications.

🔑
Section 5
App Permissions

Jobs Finder requests the following Android permissions. We only request what is necessary for the App to function.

Permission Why It's Needed Required?
INTERNET Fetching live job listings from JobMail and loading advertisements Yes
POST_NOTIFICATIONS Sending job alert notifications when new matching jobs are found Optional
RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED Restarting the background job sync after a device reboot Optional
READ_MEDIA_IMAGES Selecting a profile photo from your gallery for use in the CV builder Optional

On Android 13 and above, notification and media permissions are requested at runtime. You may decline them and the App will continue to work without those specific features.

📣
Section 6
Advertising

Jobs Finder is free to use and is supported by advertisements served by Google AdMob. These include banner ads and interstitial (full-screen) ads that appear at natural breaks in the app experience.

AdMob may use cookies and advertising identifiers to show ads that are relevant to you based on your interests and prior app usage. This is standard practice for ad-supported apps.

Opt out of personalised ads: Open your device's Google Settings → Data & Privacy → Ad Settings and toggle off "Personalise ads". You will still see ads but they will not be tailored to your interests.

Jobs Finder does not sell your personal information to advertisers.

👶
Section 7
Children's Privacy

Jobs Finder is designed for individuals who are 16 years of age or older, consistent with minimum employment age requirements in South Africa.

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has submitted personal information through the App, please contact us immediately at the address below so we can take appropriate action to remove that data.
